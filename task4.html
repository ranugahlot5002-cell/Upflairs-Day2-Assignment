Beawar

Welcome to Beawar, a historic and vibrant city located near the Ajmer district of Rajasthan.

History

Beawar was founded by Colonel Charles George Dixon in 1836. It was originally established as a military cantonment and quickly grew into a major center for the cotton trade. The city's grid-like layout is a testament to its planned origins.

Culture

The city is famous for its vibrant cultural celebrations, most notably the unique 'Badshah Mela' tied to the festival of holi. The local community is highly engaged in traditional Rajasthani arts and schooling events, such as those at Schools, Colleges and Grounds.

Food

If there is one thing Beawar is globally recognized for,cit is the famous 'Tilpatti' - a sweet delicacy made of sesame seeds and sugar. Food enthusiats travel from all over the state to enjoy this crispy, sweet treat during the winter months.

Places to Visit

Visitors frequently explore NeelKandh Mahadev, Shree Cement Balaji temple and the bustling local markets. The architectural blend of colonial and Rajasthani styles makes walking through the city center an enriching experience.
